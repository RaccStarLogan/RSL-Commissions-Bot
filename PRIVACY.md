# PRIVACY POLICY

1. Data Collected
This app does **not** collect, store, or retain personal data from any Discord user.
The only information it receives is commission form data submitted directly by the authorized user.

2. Data Usage
Commission information is used solely to generate a direct message notification to the authorized user.
No data is logged, stored, shared, or transmitted to third parties.

3. User Interaction
This app does not interact with or process data from any Discord user other than **@racc.star.logan**.

4. Data Retention
No data is retained. Once a commission notification, the app does not store any copy of the information.

5. Changes
This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed to meed Discord's verification requirements.
